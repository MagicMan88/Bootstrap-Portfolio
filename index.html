About Me

I am a Front-End Web Developer/Marketing Enthusiast with a background in Marketing, Sales, and Risk Operations. I am passionate about creating aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites that provide shared value to users and companies by providing a pleasant and pain-free experience while surfing the web.


To supplement my passion for user-friendly web-based marketing and products I earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from the University of Utah's Full-Stack Development coding boot camp. This experience enhanced my existing skillset by furthering my knowledge in HTML, JavaScript, MERN stack, CSS, SQL server and responsive web design.


In my current role, I am known for my inquisitive mind, willingness to dive head-on into difficult projects and the ability to learn quickly. All of these traits combined with my new skills make me an ideal candidate for web development positions.
